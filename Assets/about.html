About Me

Hi there! I'm Gaurav, a research student who has a quest to learn many new things and is eager to explore new places. I am pursuing my research under a joint doctoral program between Universitat Rovira I Virgili Tarragona, Spain, and the Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahabad, India. Currently, I am living in Sitges, Barcelona, Spain. My primary work is to predict human motion using deep sequential architecture. My secondary work is focused on improving the quality of life of people with intellectual and developmental disabilities. I am excited to enhance my professional expertise and skills to work managing projects through creative problem solving and inventive thinking.
